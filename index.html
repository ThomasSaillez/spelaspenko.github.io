Špela Špenko


I Since 2019 I work at Université Libre de Bruxelles, where I am now an associate professor. In the period 2017-2019 I was a PEGASUS Marie Skłodowska-Curie fellow at the Vrije Universiteit Brussel. Before I was a postdoc at University of Edinburgh. I graduated in June 2015 from the University of Ljubljana. Nowadays I work mainly in non-commutative algebraic geometry, focusing on noncommutative resolutions and homological mirror symetry.
